Abstract

This system presents a ML-based architecture designed to enhance the success rate of Phase 1 clinical trials by providing personalized diet and drug recommendations. The two-stage pipeline employs a Deep Neural Network with Batch Normalization, L2 regularization, and Dropout, complemented by an ensemble of Random Forest and Gradient Boosting classifiers using soft voting.

A dual-output recommendation engine simultaneously predicts the optimal diet plan and suitable drug class, each providing confidence scores. The system includes EDA with correlation analysis and feature importance visualization highlighting the most influential clinical parameters.
